Yacht Charter Fethiye

A gulet yacht charter in Fethiye and Gocek located in Gulf of Fethiye which is southwest of the beautiful Turkish Mediterranean coast, strewn with many islands offering a wonderful option for your vocation.

The area surrounded with many lovely bays nested together with green pine forestry, also around Fethiye (ancient name is Telmessos, named for the son of Apollo) there are important ancient cities of the Lycians i.e.; Xanthos, Karmilassos, Sidyma, Pydnai, Tlos, Letoon are some of them. Letoon was a cult center during the Lycian period and remains can be seen of temples dedicated to Leto, Artemis and Apollo. While in port during yacht charter you can visit the large and beautiful Tomb of Amyntas, built by the Lycians in 350 B.C.

Oludeniz (Blue Lagoon) also is famed for its spectacular paragliding flight from the famous mountain Babadag to the place with 3 kms of natural Blue Lagoon beach known as “a paradise that God granted to Earth”

Gocek features one of the most beautiful landscapes of twelve islands sprawled in the Gulf with wonderful coves offers an enjoyable yacht charter holiday.

Possible itineraries from the area include Rhodes, Marmaris, Ekincik while to the east Kalkan Kas and the island of Kastellorizon.

There is ferry connection from Fethiye to Rhodes daily in the summer and some days a week in the winter.

Dalaman is the closest airport 1 hour transfer time to Fethiye and only 25 minutes to Gocek.
