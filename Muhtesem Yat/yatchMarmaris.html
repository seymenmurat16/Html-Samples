Yacht Charter Marmaris


Marmaris is a town a wonder of nature surrounded by pine forests that stretch down to the waterfront, in this wonderful country of Turkey where the Aegean and the Mediterranean meets.

The ancient name of Marmaris was Physkos was a Carian city of which history goes back to the 3500 BC.

The castle of Marmaris (built by Suleyman the Magnificent in 16th century dating 1545) which overlooks to the harbour nowadays where the “Yacht Charter Show” organised every May is an enjoyable event for all nature & sea lovers.

You may have a diving, fishing or a swimming break facing to the ancient city of Amos or you can visit the beach of Sedir island (the ancient Cedrai, Cleopatra island) that said the sand of the beach brought by Alexander from Egypt for Cleopatra who was in love.(now it is a protected area)

You will have an unforgettable yacht charter holiday on board combined with history, sun, nature and sea.

For the shopaholics the shopping bazaar sells a whole range of goods and souvenirs but don’t leave without sampling the nargile (the traditional water pipe) and locally produced honey before to start your yacht charter vocation.

Marmaris has a several well established marinas and there is a daily ferry connection to Rhodes.

Possible itineraries are from Marmaris include Rhodes, Symi, Fethiye, Gocek, Datca or to Bodrum.

The nearest airport is Dalaman which is around 1 hour 30 minutes drive away by possessing the frankincense trees which are rarely found elsewhere in the world.
